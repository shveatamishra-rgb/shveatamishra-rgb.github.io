Duplicate Media Cleaner — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026.

Duplicate Media Cleaner is a macOS application that finds duplicate photos and videos on your Mac. Your privacy is simple to explain: Duplicate Media Cleaner does not collect, sell, or share any personal data.

What we collect

Nothing. Duplicate Media Cleaner has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs. We do not see your files, file names, or any usage data.

Your files stay on your device

Duplicate Media Cleaner only reads the folders you explicitly choose. It compares files by their bytes and, for images, by the pixels they decode to — entirely on your Mac. Your photos and videos are never uploaded and never leave your device. Moving a duplicate sends it to the macOS Trash, where you can recover it.

Network use

The app works fully offline. The only time it uses the network is if you send a message through the in-app support form.

Purchases

The one-time "Pro" in-app purchase is processed entirely by Apple. Duplicate Media Cleaner does not receive or store payment information.

Data retention and sharing

Because the app collects no data, there is nothing to retain, share, or sell.

Contacting us

If you message us through the support form on our website or in the app, the details you submit (your name, email, and message) are processed by our form provider, Web3Forms, and delivered to our support email. We use them only to respond to you, and we don't sell or share them. You can email us directly instead if you prefer.

Children

Duplicate Media Cleaner is suitable for all ages (rated 4+) and collects data from no one.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
