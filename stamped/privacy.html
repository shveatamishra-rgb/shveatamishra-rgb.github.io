Stamped — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026.

Stamped is a macOS application that renames and organizes your photos and videos on your Mac. Your privacy is simple to explain: Stamped does not collect, sell, or share any personal data.

What we collect

Nothing. Stamped has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs. We do not see your files, file names, locations, or any usage data.

Your files stay on your device

Stamped only reads the folders you explicitly choose. It reads date and (optionally) GPS metadata to rename and organize your media, and copies or moves the files you select. Your photos and videos are never uploaded and never leave your Mac. Undo moves files to the macOS Trash.

Location lookups (Smart Events, optional)

If you use the optional Smart Events feature, Stamped reads GPS coordinates already embedded in your media and asks Apple's geocoding service to turn those coordinates into a place name for folder naming. Only the coordinates are sent — never your photos, file contents, or file paths. No request is made if you don't use Smart Events.

Purchases

The one-time "Pro" in-app purchase is processed entirely by Apple. Stamped does not receive or store payment information.

Data retention and sharing

Because the app collects no data, there is nothing to retain, share, or sell.

Contacting us

If you message us through the support form on our website, the details you submit (your name, email, and message) are processed by our form provider, Web3Forms, and delivered to our support email. We use them only to respond to you, and we don't sell or share them. You can email us directly instead if you prefer.

Children

Stamped is suitable for all ages (rated 4+) and collects data from no one.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
