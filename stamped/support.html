Stamped — Support

Stamped renames and organizes your photos and videos by date and place, right on your Mac. Need help or have a question or bug report?

Contact

Email macosapps.help@outlook.com Please include your macOS version and a short description of what happened.

Quick answers

Why do some files have no place in the name? Smart Events needs GPS in the photo or video. Media without location lands in a date-only event folder.

Can I undo a transfer? Yes — "Undo Last Transfer" moves copies to the Trash and returns moved files to where they were.

Are my files uploaded? No. Everything runs on your Mac. Only GPS coordinates are sent to Apple's geocoder for place names, and only if you use Smart Events.
