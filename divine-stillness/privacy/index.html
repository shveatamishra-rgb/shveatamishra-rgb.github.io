Privacy Policy

Divine Stillness Om · Effective June 20, 2026

Divine Stillness Om is designed to work without an account. The app stores your favorites, preferred mantra, daily japa count, daily darshan streak, and reminder preferences locally on your device.

Information collection

The app does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal information. It does not include advertising trackers or third-party analytics. Because all of this data stays on your device, deleting the app deletes the data.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. The developer receives the purchase entitlement needed to unlock Pro features but does not receive your full payment-card details.

Notifications

If you enable a daily darshan reminder, notification permission is used only to schedule that local reminder. It can be disabled in the app or in iPhone Settings.

Photos

Photo-library add permission is requested only when you choose to save a devotional wallpaper. The app does not read your photo library.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

This policy will be updated before adding analytics, accounts, cloud sync, advertising, or any other data collection.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to divine.stillness.om@gmail.com.
