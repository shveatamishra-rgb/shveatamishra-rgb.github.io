GigaBack Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 July 2026

GigaBack is private by design. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

What GigaBack does

GigaBack scans the photo library on your iPhone to find duplicate photos, similar shots, screenshots, blurry photos, and large videos, so you can review them and free up storage. All scanning and matching happens entirely on your device.

Data we collect

None. GigaBack has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no tracking, and no telemetry. We do not operate any server. Your photos, videos, and scan results never leave your iPhone.

Photo library access

GigaBack asks for photo library access only to scan for duplicates and clutter, show you the results, and remove the items you choose. You can also grant limited access and scan only the photos you select. Photos are analyzed on your device and are never uploaded anywhere.

Deleting photos

Nothing is ever removed without your confirmation. Deletions go through the standard iOS Photos confirmation, and everything you delete stays in your Recently Deleted album for about 30 days, where you can recover it.

Imported files

If you import image files from the Files app (for example, chat exports), they are copied only into GigaBack's private storage on your device so they can be scanned. You can clear them at any time from inside the app.

iCloud photos

If your library uses iCloud Photos with optimized storage, scanning may cause your device to download originals from your own iCloud account through Apple. That traffic is between your device and Apple; we never see it.

Purchases

GigaBack Pro subscriptions and the lifetime unlock are handled entirely by Apple through your Apple Account. We do not receive or store your payment details.

Children

GigaBack is not directed at children and collects no data from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the date above.

Contact

Questions and data requests: use the GigaBack contact form. Messages reach us directly; we usually reply within a day or two.
